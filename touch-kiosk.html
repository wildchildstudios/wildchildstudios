Touch Kiosk

  1. Home
  2. Technologies
  3. Touch Kiosk

It is always good to give people a reason to stop and learn new things. Touch Kiosk which are very common at railway stations to know about the train schedules but the same kiosks can do wonders and perform much more than just telling people about the schedules. We can make people aware about the history, wildlife, forest, heritage sites with the help of kiosks using AV presentations and quiz. Touch kiosks are commonly used for information collection and engaging people. It is based on touch device which would respond to touch and will give some unusual experience to the user.

We at WildChild Studios work on the various aspects of the touch kiosks. We design the interactive designs, quiz, surveys, games and AV presentations for touch kiosks. We make sure that our clients get the maximum data from the kiosk and benefited by the analytical reports of information collected.
