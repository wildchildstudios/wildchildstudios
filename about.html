About Company

WildChild Studios is a name of team came together for a common purpose of delivering the best quality creative solutions for various events and industries. We at WildChild Studios work to make an ordinary event, presentation, museum or show an amazingly nurturing experience for the people.

Our team has experienced creative technology masters who work day and night to create concepts that can be the best fit for your event. All our creative solutions are driven through latest technology and great sense of creative artists be it a projection mapping, Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, Game development or 3D stereoscopic film production. We have been involved in a constant technological upgrade process to keep updated and bring the best to our clients. Our team is a dynamic blend of seasoned professionals and young, passionate individuals who are eager to learn, contribute, and grow. Whether through hands-on support at on-site projects or by actively participating in creative concept development in the office, every team member plays a vital role in driving innovation and excellence.

We have been fortunate enough to work with many corporates, government bodies, religious mandlis, event organizers and political parties to shape their events, promotions and product launches a great success.
